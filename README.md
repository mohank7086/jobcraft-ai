# JobCraft AI 🚀

**Generate ATS-optimised resumes, cover letters, and outreach messages in seconds.**

No login. No setup. Just paste your resume + job description and go.

## How to use
1. Clone or download this repo.
2. Open html file directly in your browser.
3. No server or install needed — runs fully in browser.
4. Upload your resume (PDF).
5. Paste the job description.
6. Get your ATS-optimised resume, cover letter, and outreach message instantly in pdf format available for download. 

---

## What it does

Every job posting is different. Recruiters use ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) to filter
resumes before a human ever sees them. JobCraft AI solves this by tailoring your entire
application to the specific JD you're targeting — in seconds.

**Input:**
- Your existing resume
- The job description of the role you're applying for

**Output:**
- ✅ ATS-optimised resume (2-layer ATS score check)
- ✅ Tailored cover letter
- ✅ LinkedIn / email outreach message

The best-scoring resume version is automatically selected based on ATS analysis.

---

## Why it matters

- Applying to 10 roles = 10 different JDs = 10 different resumes needed
- Doing this manually takes hours
- JobCraft AI does it in **seconds**, fully optimised for each JD
